Three-Level Agent Architecture

TwinSphere's revolutionary approach to digital twins is built on a sophisticated three-level agent architecture that brings intelligence and autonomy to digital replicas.

Our platform transforms the digital twin paradigm from static, passive representations to dynamic, intelligent entities capable of autonomous operation. This transformation is achieved through our innovative agent-based architecture that combines specialized AI agents with advanced integration capabilities and intuitive user interfaces.

Level 1: Interaction Agents

These specialized agents interface directly with devices, systems, and data sources, collecting real-time information and executing commands. They handle the bidirectional flow of data between the physical/digital world and the twin environment.

Level 2: Orchestration Agents

These agents coordinate activities, validate tasks, and manage execution workflows. They ensure that the right information is routed to the appropriate reasoning agents and that actions are properly executed by interaction agents.

Level 3: Reasoning Agents

These advanced AI agents analyze data, identify patterns, make predictions, and determine optimal actions. They leverage large language models and specialized AI to provide the cognitive capabilities that drive the digital twin's autonomous behavior.
